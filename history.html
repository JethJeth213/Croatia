Key Historical Moments

Croatia's history dates back to ancient times, from the Illyrians to the Romans, and later the medieval Kingdom of Croatia.

  • Medieval Kingdom: Formed in the 10th century
  • Part of the Austro-Hungarian Empire: From the 19th century
  • Independence: Gained in 1991 after the breakup of Yugoslavia
