Fun Facts About Croatia

  • Croatia invented the necktie (cravat).
  • The Dalmatian dog breed originates from Dalmatia, a region in Croatia.
  • Croatia has over 1,200 islands, islets, and reefs.
  • Game of Thrones filmed many scenes in Dubrovnik.
  • One of the smallest towns in the world, Hum, is in Croatia.
