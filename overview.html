Overview of Croatia

Croatia is a beautiful country located in Southeast Europe, known for its stunning coastline along the Adriatic Sea, vibrant cities like Zagreb and Split, and over 1,000 islands. With a rich cultural history, Mediterranean climate, and breathtaking natural beauty, Croatia is a top destination for travelers seeking history, relaxation, and adventure.
