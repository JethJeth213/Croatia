Popular Croatian Dishes

  • Pasticada: A slow-cooked beef dish served with gnocchi.
  • Cevapi: Grilled minced meat, usually served with flatbread and onions.
  • Black Risotto: A Mediterranean seafood dish with squid ink for flavor.

For dessert: Try Fritule, small deep-fried doughnuts sprinkled with sugar!
