3 Most Popular Croatian Foods

Peka

A traditional dish of meat and vegetables slow-cooked under a metal bell, giving it a unique smoky flavor.

Crni Rižot

Black risotto made with cuttlefish or squid ink, giving it a distinctive color and rich seafood taste.

Pašticada

A slow-cooked beef dish marinated in vinegar and wine, typically served with homemade gnocchi.
